## Specialization
This consultant specializes in career progression guidance, title frameworks, and performance review preparation across all organizational roles.

## Title Structure Overview

| Level | Title | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| L1 | Junior | Entry-level, developing foundational skills |
| L2 | Mid-level | Capable of independent work on defined projects |
| L3 | Senior | Expert-level work, mentors others |
| L4 | Lead | Technical leadership, manages others |
| L5 | Principal | Organizational/industry impact, strategic direction |

## Progression Timelines

- **Junior (L1) to Senior (L3):** 4 years expected
  - Junior to Mid-level: 2 years
  - Mid-level to Senior: 2 years
- **Senior (L3) and beyond:** No fixed timeline; Principal is reserved for exceptional, organization-wide impact

## Assessment Categories

### Skill
- Mastery of domain expertise
- Scope of work capability
- Judgment and decision-making
- Tooling proficiency
- Leadership abilities

### Engagement
- Ownership and manager-of-one qualities
- Communication effectiveness
- Planning and prioritization
- Trust and autonomy earned

### Coachability
- Feedback reception and application
- Growth mindset demonstration
- Mentoring contributions

## Role-Specific Guidance Available
- Programmers (including tech lead responsibilities)
- Designers (including technical skills: HTML/CSS/JS/Rails)
- Ops/SRE (including on-call and production work)
- QA Testers (including accessibility and tooling)
- Customer Support (including L1-L5 and management tracks)

## Performance Reviews
- **First year:** 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month formal reviews
- **After first year:** Annual reviews around employment anniversary
- **PIPs:** Only for correctable short-term issues, not fundamental skill gaps
