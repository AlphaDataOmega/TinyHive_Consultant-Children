# Employee Handbook Specialist Documentation

## Overview

This directory contains reference materials and procedural documentation for the Employee Handbook Specialist agent. The specialist provides guidance on all aspects of employee workplace policies, onboarding, benefits, and operational procedures.

## Core Knowledge Domains

### 1. Getting Started / Onboarding
- Pre-start preparation and equipment ordering
- Operating system requirements by role
- First day procedures and account setup
- First 90 days expectations and milestones
- Key contacts: Manager, Ops Buddy, Company Buddy, People Operations

### 2. How We Work
- Remote work policy for distributed teams
- Six-week work cycle structure with cooldown periods
- Communication requirements (daily/weekly check-ins, kickoffs, heartbeats)
- Asynchronous work principles
- Self-management expectations ("managers of one")
- Work-life balance including summer hours (32-hour weeks May-August)

### 3. Benefits and Perks
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision, life, disability)
- Retirement plans (401K with company match, international equivalents)
- Flexible Spending Accounts (Healthcare and Dependent Care)
- Paid Time Off (20 days + 11 holidays, rollover policies)
- Sabbatical (6 weeks every 3 years)
- Family leave (16 weeks primary, 6 weeks secondary caregiver)
- Bereavement leave
- Employee profit sharing and liquidity pool programs
- Equipment and workspace benefits ($3,000 home office, $200/month coworking)
- Expense account management

### 4. Code of Conduct
- Core expectations (assume good intentions, transparency, respect, feedback)
- Anti-discrimination and anti-harassment policies
- Reporting procedures (standard and anonymous)
- Consequences of violations
- Retaliation prohibition
- Interpersonal conflicts vs. Code of Conduct reports
- Politics at work guidelines

### 5. Device Management
- macOS management via Kandji
- Linux management via Omarchy
- Access to code and secrets requirements
- Personal data on work devices policy
- Unmanaged device guidelines (iOS, Android, Windows)

### 6. Moonlighting Policy
- Permitted activities (side gigs, speaking, advisory roles, volunteer work)
- Prohibited activities (competing companies, industry consulting)
- Evaluation criteria for outside work

### 7. Internal Systems
- Queenbee (billing and customer management)
- Sentry (exception tracking)
- Grafana (monitoring and dashboards)
- Dash (request logging and reporting)
- Kandji (macOS device management)
- Omarchy (in-house Linux distribution)

### 8. Company Rituals
- Bi-annual company meet-ups
- All-hands meetings (end of each 6-week cycle)
- Everyone on Support (EOS) program

### 9. Severance Packages
- Standard severance terms by tenure
- Additional benefits (health insurance continuation, vacation payout)
- Exclusions for termination for cause

### 10. State Leave Provisions
- California (Disability Insurance, Paid Family Leave)
- Colorado (Parental Bonding, Family Care, Self Care, Military Family, Safe Leave)
- Massachusetts (Medical Leave, Family Care, Bonding, Military Family)
- New Jersey (Temporary Disability, Maternity Coverage, Family Leave Insurance)
- Oregon (Medical Leave, Bonding, Family Care, Safe Leave)

## Usage Guidelines

When providing guidance:
1. Reference specific handbook sections for policy questions
2. Direct employees to People Operations for binding interpretations
3. Consider state-specific provisions when applicable
4. Maintain confidentiality of all employee information
5. Escalate legal questions to qualified employment attorneys
