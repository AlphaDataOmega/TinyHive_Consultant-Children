# Food Safety Manager Documentation

This directory contains reference materials and procedural guidance for the Food Safety Manager consultant.

## Core Knowledge Areas

### Time/Temperature Control for Safety (TCS) Foods

TCS foods require time/temperature control to prevent pathogenic microorganism growth or toxin formation.

**TCS Food Categories:**
- **Animal Foods:** Milk products, meats, shell eggs, fish, poultry, shellfish
- **Heat-Treated Plant Foods:** Cooked onions, rice, soy protein, potatoes
- **Raw Plant Foods:** Cut leafy greens, cut tomatoes, cut melons, raw seed sprouts
- **Other:** Garlic-in-oil mixtures, sauces/pastries containing TCS ingredients

### Mandatory SOPs (All Establishments)

1. **Handwashing** - Proper technique and required timing
2. **Personal Hygiene** - Clothing, wound coverage, jewelry, hair restraints
3. **Bare Hand Contact** - Prevention for ready-to-eat foods
4. **Employee Health** - Symptom recognition, reporting, exclusion/restriction
5. **Food from Approved Sources** - Receiving and inspection procedures
6. **Warewashing** - Manual and mechanical cleaning/sanitizing
7. **Protecting Food from Contamination** - Storage, separation, handling

### Conditional SOPs (When Applicable)

- Date Marking Ready-to-Eat TCS Food
- Time as a Public Health Control
- Thawing TCS Food
- Cooking TCS Food
- Cooling TCS Food
- Reheating for Hot Holding
- Hot Holding TCS Food
- Cold Holding TCS Food
- Catering/Off-Site/Satellite Food Service
- Outdoor Exposed Dining/Food Preparation

### HACCP Plans

Required for specialized processes:
- Smoking food for preservation
- Curing
- Reduced oxygen packaging
- Fermentation
- Packaging raw unpasteurized juice

## Quick Reference: Temperature Requirements

| Process | Temperature | Time |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Cold holding | 41F or less | Continuous |
| Hot holding | 135F or more | Continuous |
| Cooking - Poultry/stuffed items | 165F | 15 seconds |
| Cooking - Ground meats/eggs | 155F | 15 seconds |
| Cooking - Fish/meat/eggs (immediate service) | 145F | 15 seconds |
| Cooking - Vegetables for hot holding | 135F | 15 seconds |
| Reheating for hot holding | 165F | 15 seconds |
| Reheating commercial RTE | 135F | - |
| Cooling (cooked food) | 135F to 70F | 2 hours |
| Cooling (cooked food) | 135F to 41F | 6 hours total |
| Cooling (ambient/pre-chilled) | To 41F | 4 hours |

## Employee Health: Big Five Pathogens

Pathogens requiring immediate exclusion upon diagnosis:
1. Norovirus
2. Hepatitis A virus
3. Shigella spp.
4. Enterohemorrhagic or Shiga toxin-producing E. coli
5. Salmonella typhi

## SOP Documentation Requirements

Each SOP must include:
1. Procedure Description
2. Responsible Position(s)
3. Monitoring Methods
4. Corrective Actions
5. Training Methods
6. Applicable Logs
