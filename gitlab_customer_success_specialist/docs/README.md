## Core Knowledge Domains

### PROVE Health Scoring Framework
| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Product | 50% | Feature adoption, usage metrics |
| Risk | 25% | Churn indicators, escalations |
| Outcomes | 10% | Business goal achievement |
| Voice of Customer | 5% | Satisfaction, NPS |
| Engagement | 10% | Meeting attendance, responsiveness |

### Health Status Definitions
- **Green**: Healthy, no intervention needed
- **Yellow**: At risk, proactive engagement required
- **Red**: Critical, immediate action plan needed
