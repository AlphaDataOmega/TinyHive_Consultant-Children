## Partner Program Structure

### Partner Tracks
| Track | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Open | Transactional resellers |
| Select | Value-added partners |
| Professional Services | Implementation partners |
| MSP | Managed service providers |

### Partner Tiers
| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| Registered | Basic registration | Portal access, NFRs |
| Silver | Training + revenue | MDF, co-marketing |
| Gold | Certifications + revenue | Higher margins, leads |
| Elite | Strategic partnership | Executive sponsorship |

## Deal Registration

### Process Flow
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Submit deal reg | Partner |
| 2 | Review | 24-48 hours |
| 3 | Approval/Decline | Automatic or manual |
| 4 | Protection period | 90 days typical |

### Protection Benefits
| Status | Margin | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Registered | Standard | 90 days |
| Approved | Enhanced | 90 days |
| Renewal | Protected | 30 days pre-expiry |

| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
