# Company Values Specialist Documentation

## Overview

This directory contains reference materials and documentation for the Company Values Specialist consultant agent. The agent specializes in organizational values definition, mission alignment, culture development, and policy governance processes.

## Core Knowledge Domains

### 1. Mission Statement Development

- Articulating organizational purpose and direction
- Connecting mission to strategy and operations
- Communicating mission effectively to stakeholders
- Aligning individual roles with organizational mission
- Reviewing and updating mission as organization evolves

### 2. Core Values Framework

#### Continuous Improvement
- "Be better today than yesterday" philosophy
- Progress over perfection mindset
- Ownership of mistakes and learning
- Questioning habits and assumptions
- Regular reflection and retrospectives

#### Empathy and Respect
- Treating others as they wish to be treated
- Considering diverse perspectives
- Building stakeholder understanding
- Active listening practices
- Accommodation of different needs

#### Inclusion and Diversity
- Fighting the default of exclusion
- Diverse team building as moral imperative
- Seeking voices unlike your own
- Bias identification and reduction
- Equitable policy development

#### Trust and Transparency
- Trust as currency of team interactions
- Candid feedback enabling creativity
- Deep visibility into decisions
- Open input channels
- Communication building future trust

## Reference Framework

### Values Assessment Questions

| Value | Assessment Questions |
|-------|---------------------|
| Continuous Improvement | Are we better than last month? What have we learned? |
| Empathy | Have we considered all perspectives? Are we listening? |
| Inclusion | Are diverse voices represented? Who is excluded? |
| Trust | Are we transparent? Do people feel safe speaking up? |

### Policy Change Checklist

1. Identify need and gather evidence
2. Draft proposal with rationale
3. Schedule discussion meeting
4. Collect and incorporate feedback
5. Obtain necessary approvals
6. Merge changes to authoritative source
7. Update tracking systems
8. Communicate to affected parties
9. Schedule acknowledgment collection

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Core Values | Fundamental beliefs guiding organizational behavior and decision-making |
| Mission Statement | Declaration of organizational purpose and primary objectives |
| Living Document | Policy designed for continuous evolution and improvement |
| Values Alignment | Consistency between stated values and actual practices |
| Policy Governance | Systems for developing, reviewing, and updating organizational policies |
| Inclusion | Active effort to ensure all stakeholders can participate and belong |
| Transparency | Open sharing of information, reasoning, and decision processes |

## Usage Guidelines

This agent should be consulted for:
- Values discovery and articulation
- Mission statement development
- Culture assessment and development
- Policy governance design
- Change management processes
- Inclusion and diversity initiatives
- Values integration into HR processes
- Communication strategy for values

Refer to specialized consultants for:
- Employment law compliance (HR Policy Specialist)
- Benefits and compensation specifics (Benefits Perks Specialist)
- Specific employment policies (Employment Policy Specialist)
- Legal review of policies (Employment Counsel)
